# Brand Applications

> Phase: identity | Brand: ShieldPay | Generated: 2026-06-27

---

How the evolved identity reads in context. Across every surface the dials hold:
calm (Boldness 2), dense-but-legible (Density 4), exact (Precision 5), restrained
physical motion (Motion 2), cool (Warmth 2). The gradient is the only ornament,
and it always means protected, then proven.

## Company portal (CFO dashboard)

The flagship surface. Page on `surface-base` with the low ambient gradient. The
dashboard leads with one hero figure (operating balance or paid this month) in
Geist Mono, tabular, carrying the gentle indigo to emerald wash; it is the one
number that glows. Everything else is neutral. The primary card cluster sits on
`surface-1` with the hairline border and a faint indigo top-edge rim.

Below it, the dense verified payments table: `surface-2` header, 52px standard
rows, currency right-aligned in mono, the four text tiers carrying hierarchy
(provider in `fg-strong`, reference and date in `fg-subtle`). Each settled row
shows the emerald verified badge with its proof ID. Hover raises a row to
`surface-3`; a selected row gets the `border-strong` edge. This is density as
legitimacy, calm and scannable, no color noise.

## Worker portal (the contributor)

The one place we let a little warmth in, expressed as discretion and clarity, not
illustration. The moment that matters is "you were paid, and here is the proof."
A contributor lands on a single clear confirmation: the period, the emerald
verified badge, and the proof ID in mono, set on `surface-1` with the verified
glow behind the badge. The exact amount is the contributor's own, shown plainly to
them in tabular mono (they are the owner, not an outside viewer). The tone is
reassurance through exactness: short, precise, no jargon, "Verified on-chain," not
"Groth16." Download-the-receipt is one calm primary action in indigo.

## Auditor portal (read-only, disclosed)

The disclosure surface. Accessed by a scoped, expiring link, no wallet. This is
where masked becomes disclosed: the exact figures the rest of the system keeps
private are revealed here, in tabular mono, right-aligned, for the auditor the
company authorized. The reveal carries the brief indigo to emerald reveal motion
the first time a figure is disclosed, then rests. A persistent, calm banner states
the access is read-only and time-bound. No balance, no controls, no funds
movement, only the period's proven payments laid out as an exact, exportable
ledger. The Steward, keeping clean books, handing over math.

## The payroll run stepper (the trust moment)

The one place the motion budget is spent. A discrete, stepped sequence:
sending, proving, verifying, settled. Each stage eases in over the base duration
with the physical ease; the connector fills indigo to emerald as it advances from
protected to proven; the final settled state lands with a subtle emerald confirm.
No spinner, no loop. The whole sequence animates the brand's core meaning in the
moment the CFO most needs to trust it. Under `prefers-reduced-motion`, it drops to
instant state swaps with no loss of meaning.

## The verified badge

The emerald confirmation, used scarcely so it stays an event. Emerald wash field,
emerald line border, the lucide ShieldCheck functional icon, and the tabular proof
ID beside it. It confirms correctness without revealing the amount. It sits at
elevation level 2 and is never used decoratively or for general success toasts;
it means a payment was proven on-chain.

## The masked to verified to disclosed amount component

The signature, as a reusable component with three first-class states. Masked: the
designed slate chip with the indigo protected cue (never asterisks or blur).
Verified: the emerald badge plus proof ID, correctness without the number.
Disclosed: the real tabular mono figure, revealed with the indigo to emerald
motion for an authorized viewer. The same component expresses the whole promise,
"the amount stays yours, the proof is everyone's," in one place.

## PDF court-receipt header

The formal, off-chain artifact, and the place the monochrome mark lives. Header
carries the single-ink slit shield (gradient cannot be relied on in print), the
ShieldPay wordmark in Inter, and the document's hashes, ledger number, and proof
ID in mono, tabular, exactly formatted. The receipt reads as a precise legal
instrument: calm, exact, no decoration. This is the Steward's output a judge or
auditor can check.

## Landing hero

The one marketing surface. Dark canvas with the ambient gradient, the tagline
"Private payroll you can prove." in Inter Display, and a single restrained hero
showing the masked to verified to disclosed motion or a calm dashboard frame. No
stock photos, no crypto imagery, no neon. The visual motif line "Protected, then
proven." can anchor the gradient. Bold position, quiet surface: the headline makes
the claim, the design stays calm enough that a skeptical CFO believes it.
