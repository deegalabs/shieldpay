## The strategy in one screen

- **Category:** Provable privacy / confidential payroll. Own the seam between
  public-ledger payroll (exposes salaries) and trust-me payroll (proves nothing).
- **Positioning statement:** For finance teams at DAOs and Web3-native companies
  who must pay contributors in stablecoins without publishing every salary,
  ShieldPay is the confidential payroll platform that keeps each amount private
  while proving on-chain that it was paid correctly, disclosed only to an auditor
  the company authorizes, because every payment is settled on Stellar and backed by
  a proof verified in a smart contract, not by ShieldPay's word.
- **The "only" line:** ShieldPay is the only payroll where each salary stays
  private and every payment is proven correct on-chain, disclosed solely to the
  auditor you choose.
- **Differentiator:** the masked → verified → disclosed sequence, made real end to
  end. No competitor owns all three as one settlement-grade motion.
- **Archetype:** Guardian / Steward. Trust through restraint and care, not swagger.
- **Voice:** Calm · Precise · Trustworthy. No crypto jargon, ever.
- **Tagline (pick):** Private payroll you can prove.
- **Intensity dials** (drive identity + design system): Boldness 2, Density 4,
  Legibility/Precision 5, Motion 2, Warmth 2, Ornamentation 1, Contrast 4,
  Saturation 2. Net: calm + high craft, not flashy.
