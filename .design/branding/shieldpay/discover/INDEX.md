## Headline moves

- **Replace flat surfaces with a 5-step lightness-based elevation ramp** (Mercury
  / Coinbase / Linear model: depth by surface lightness, not shadow). Biggest
  perceived-quality jump, lowest equity risk.
- **Apply tabular + mono to every figure, hash, and proof ID**, and insert a mid
  text tier so the 98%ŌåÆ62% jump becomes a 4-rung scale (Stripe / Ramp).
- **Re-deploy the indigoŌåÆemerald gradient as dimensional light** (hero figure,
  surface edges, focused fields, stepper), not a flat text fill.
- **Adopt Geist motion tokens** for a stepped, physical payroll
  provingŌåÆverifyingŌåÆsettled confirm-sequence.
- **Invent the masked ŌåÆ verified ŌåÆ disclosed signature** as the un-claimed
  privacy visual language.
