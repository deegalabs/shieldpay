Voice

Calm, Precise, Trustworthy

Never

  • Crypto jargon. Say "Verified on-chain", not "Groth16".
  • Em-dashes, exclamation marks, or emoji in product copy.
  • A lock emoji for verified. The badge is designed.
  • Asterisks or blur for a masked amount.
  • Indigo-500 as body text. Use indigo-400 for text.
  • The gradient as a flat fill, paint, or text decoration.

Always

  • Short, exact sentences.
  • Tabular mono on every amount, hash, and ID.
  • Right-align currency so decimals stack.
  • Format exactly: $500.00 USDC, ledger 58,204,113, Proof ID 789.
  • Reserve emerald for confirmation.
  • Elevation by lightness and a 1px top-edge light.
